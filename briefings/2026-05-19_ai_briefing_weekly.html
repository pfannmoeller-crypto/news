EU May 7 · in effect this week
EU Council and Parliament agree to simplify the AI Act — Aug 2026 deadline holds
A provisional political agreement to streamline the AI Act. Key effects: the August 2, 2026 full-application date holds for the transparency regime. The grace period for providers to implement transparency for AI-generated content was cut from 6 months to 3 months — new deadline December 2, 2026. National regulatory sandbox setup is pushed to August 2, 2027. For DACH operators: expect compliance scoping conversations to harden over the summer; the simplification reduces some scope but advances enforcement timing on AI-generated content disclosure.
