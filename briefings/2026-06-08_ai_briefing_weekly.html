EU / Policy 3 June
EU AI Act transparency consultation closes; Code of Practice due this month ahead of 2 August deadline
The Commission’s targeted consultation on Article 50 transparency guidelines closed 3 June. The voluntary Code of Practice on marking and labelling AI-generated content is expected to be finalized in June, ahead of the transparency obligations becoming applicable on 2 August 2026 — providers must disclose AI interaction and add machine-readable marks to synthetic content; deployers must label deepfakes and emotion/biometric systems. Separately, a May political push to simplify and possibly delay parts of the Act still needs Council sign-off; watch whether that lands before August.
